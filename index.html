About Us

Your Health, Our Priority

Sara Life Sciences was established in the year 2014 in Hyderabad
as a small startup buisness.The founder of the company is
Late Mohammed Jalaluddin Razzaqui who was a highly reputable pharmacist.
He was widely recognized for his dedication and invaluable assistance in
the field.

Sara Life Sciences is now led by his belovd wife, Mrs. Sameena Parveen.
Our main goal is to provide Medicals and Pharmaceuticals at your doorstep.
We provide a wide variety of high quality medical supplies at Wholesale price. We supply medications all over India and a quick home delivery for Hyderabad residents.

Our shop is located at Tolichowki in the beautiful city of Hyderabad.
We give exciting offers and whooping discounts to customers who visit our store and make purchases.

Our staff is well trained and posess extensive expertise in the field of pharmacy. We also offer home delivery for locals residing in Hyderabad. Our operating hours are Monday to Saturday, from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM.We occasionally accept emergency orders on Sundays aswell.

Do visit us to shop the best Surgicals and Pharmaceuticals in India.
